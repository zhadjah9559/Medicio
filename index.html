    About Us

    Joshua Konoff started his professional career as a Real Estate Sales specialist, then went into Business Development, and now has his sights set on the Machine Learning industry. In 2021, he created Machine Learning for Good as a way to share his hobby for Machine Learning.

    His goal was to:

    • Educate the general public on how to properly utilize Machine Learning to benefit society.
    • Pass on knowledge to anyone (newcomers or established developers) who has an interest in the tech industry.
    • Advance medical technology to help both medical professionals and patients in need.
